Our Trip to Chef Ho's

After our class trip to Chef Ho's for a Chinese New year lunch, we wrote this book about the experience. Each of us wrote and illustrated one page in the book. in our writing, we each incorporated at least one of Ezra Jack Keats' techniques. See if you can find them!
